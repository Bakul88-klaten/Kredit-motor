# Kredit Motor Klaten Website Development

---
## Task ID: 1 - main-developer
### Work Task
Create a comprehensive marketing website for "Kredit Motor Klaten" with all required sections and interactive features.

### Work Summary
Successfully created a complete marketing website for Kredit Motor Klaten with the following components:

1. **Hero Section**: Eye-catching hero with motor background image, strong headline, promo badges, and WhatsApp CTA button.

2. **Promo Spesial Section**: 4 promo cards (Gratis Angsuran 1x, Cicilan Rendah, Gratis Helm, Diskon Khusus) with attractive gradients and icons.

3. **Motor Showcase Section**: Tabbed interface for Honda (blue theme) and Yamaha (red theme) with motor cards, prices, and WhatsApp inquiry buttons.

4. **Keunggulan Section**: 6 advantage cards explaining why customers should choose Kredit Motor Klaten.

5. **Testimoni Section**: Customer testimonials with star ratings and profile images.

6. **CTA Section**: Strong call-to-action with WhatsApp button and contact info.

7. **Footer**: Complete business information, contact details, and copyright.

8. **Interactive Features**:
   - Floating WhatsApp button (always visible)
   - Scroll-to-top button
   - Smooth animations with Framer Motion
   - Mobile-responsive navigation with hamburger menu
   - Hover effects on cards and buttons
   - Entrance animations on scroll

**Technologies Used**: Next.js 15, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion, shadcn/ui components

**Images**: All images from /download/ folder copied to /public/images/ for optimal serving.
